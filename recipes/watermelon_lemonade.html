Watermelon Lemonade

Description

This watermelon lemonade is a refreshing and delicious summer drink that combines the sweet juiciness of watermelon with the tangy zest of fresh lemons. Perfect for hot days, this homemade lemonade is easy to make and can be enjoyed by the whole family.

Ingredients

    4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
    1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4-6 lemons)
    1/2 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
    4 cups cold water
    Ice cubes
    Lemon slices and mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the cubed watermelon and lemon juice. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a large pitcher to remove any pulp. Use a spoon to press down on the solids to extract as much liquid as possible.
  3. Add the granulated sugar to the pitcher and stir until it is completely dissolved.
  4. Pour in the cold water and stir well to combine. Taste and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar if desired.
  5. Refrigerate the watermelon lemonade for at least 30 minutes to chill before serving.
  6. Serve over ice cubes and garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves if desired. Enjoy!
